What is PathFinder?

Pathfinder is a tool for IT job market exploring. With PathFinder we can answer this kind of questions:

  • How the IT job market behaves?
  • What the job market needs?
  • How a student could enter job market?
  • Which are the top companies?
  • How plan a career path?

PathFinder allow us to explore the market in terms of job positions, companies, salaries, job skills and even the time intelligence needed for planning all kind of experiments.

We are trying to access IT job market with IT job market data.

How did it start?

This tool started as a solution for hiring/recruitment staff. The original purpose was saving time while scrolling down job web pages. With this approach, the first version was born. From the moment this feature was achieved, we made the next steps for analyzing the whole IT job market. Now we can access positions data, companies data, skills data and much more.

Our Goal

Our path is to centralize all IT job market data in just one place.

  • With the job market data in our hand we can analyze and improve all our market actions.
  • With companies' data in our hands we can achieve best partners.
  • With skills data in our hands we can instruct people with the necessary capabilities to enter the market.

Our purpose is to have all this tools in one click..
